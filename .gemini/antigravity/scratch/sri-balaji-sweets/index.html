Our Heritage & Craft

From a small sweet stall in 1978 to a hallmark of celebration, Sri Balaji Ghee Sweets has stayed true to its ancestral recipes and unmatched quality.

Every batch of our Kaju Katli, Dry Fruit Laddu, Mysore Pak, Ghee Laddu, and Badam Halwa is prepared under strict quality control. We source milk from organic dairy farms, cardamom from the hills of Kerala, and Kashmiri saffron to create a taste that lingers forever.
